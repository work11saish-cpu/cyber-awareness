SDG 4 Mapping - Quality Education

CyberLearn aligns with SDG 4 by integrating digital literacy and cybersecurity awareness into education:

  • Provides free cybersecurity lessons accessible to all students.
  • Encourages responsible digital behavior and safe online practices.
  • Enhances knowledge of digital threats like phishing, malware, and scams.
  • Supports interactive learning through quizzes, games, and feedback.
  • Empowers students with practical digital skills for modern education.
