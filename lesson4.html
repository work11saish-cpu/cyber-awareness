CyberLearn – Lesson 4

Device & Network Safety

Your mobile, laptop, and WiFi are gateways to your personal data. Keeping them secure protects you from malware, hacking, and data theft.

Always install software updates, avoid using public WiFi without a VPN, and never download unknown apps or files.

Use antivirus software, strong device passwords, and lock your device when not in use.
