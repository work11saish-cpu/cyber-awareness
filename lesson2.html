CyberLearn – Lesson 2

Phishing Awareness

Phishing is a cyberattack where hackers pretend to be trusted companies or people to steal your personal information. They often send fake emails, messages, or links that look real.

Always check the sender’s email address and avoid clicking unknown links. Look for spelling mistakes, unusual requests, or urgent messages forcing you to act quickly.

Never share OTPs, passwords, or bank details. When in doubt, contact the official website or support team directly.
