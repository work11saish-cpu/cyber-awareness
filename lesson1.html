CyberLearn – Lesson 1

Strong Passwords

A strong password is the most basic yet powerful way to protect your accounts. Always use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.

Avoid using personal details like your name, birthday, or phone number. Use passwords like Rk!94@zpL#72 instead of saish123.

The longer and more random your password is, the harder it is for hackers to crack.
