CyberLearn – Lesson 3

Social Media Safety

Social media platforms collect a lot of your personal information. If not handled carefully, it can be misused by hackers, stalkers, or scammers.

Always keep your account private, enable two-factor authentication, and avoid posting your location in real-time. Be careful with unknown friend requests and suspicious DMs.

Avoid oversharing. The less personal information you upload, the safer you remain online.
